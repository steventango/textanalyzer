Instructions

To Analyze SMS Data: Upload a file in CSV format or paste your csv data into the text box.
The csv format is Subject(Number/Email), Date(YY-MM-DD HH:MM (AM or PM)), Subject, Country Code, "Text"

Browser Support

Text Analyzer supports the last two versions of every major browser. Specifically, Text Analyzer is tested with Chrome 60, and iOS 10 Safari.
